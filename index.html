I Worked In AUTOMATIC SOIL IRRIGATION Project It is an minor project

Project Overview

An automatic soil irrigation system aims to enhance agricultural efficiency and water conservation by providing precise moisture management. This system, designed without relying on Arduino or similar microcontrollers, leverages simple electronic components such as soil moisture sensors, relays, and timers. The soil moisture sensors monitor the water content in the soil, and when it falls below a pre-set threshold, the relay activates a water pump, initiating irrigation. Once the soil reaches the desired moisture level, the relay deactivates the pump, stopping the irrigation. This automated approach minimizes water usage, ensures optimal soil moisture for crop growth, and reduces the need for manual intervention, ultimately contributing to sustainable agricultural practices..
